Verbatim extraction & correction interface

During my internship at LS2N lab, I had the opportunity to create from scratch both the backend and frontend of an application for meeting automatic transcription and correction.

The meeting can be provided either as audio or video and its audio content is fully transcripted thanks to Whisper [1] model. All the speakers of the audio are detected and separated thanks to Pyannote [2] to fit the closest to a human audio transcription with multiple speakers. The program can recognize the speakers accross audio extracts and keeps speakers data in a database.

The web app allows to visualize the previously processed results (transcription) and to listen again to the meeting at the same time of reading the transcription so the user can correct easily the verbatim. The web app features multi-user (concurrent) transcription visualization and correction so multiple people can edit at the same time.

[1] - Alec Radford et al. Robust Speech Recognition via Large-Scale Weak Supervision. 2022. arXiv : 2212.04356 [eess.AS].
[2] - Hervé Bredin et al. « pyannote.audio : neural building blocks for speaker diarization ». In : ICASSP 2020, IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing. 2020.
