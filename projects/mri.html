Breast cancer detection on MRIs

During my internship at Hera-Mi, I had the opportunity to work on breast MRIs processing for detecting breast cancer. The given intial objective was first to detect if an MRI contained one or more malignant cancer (classification) and then to locate them (segmentation) if possible. From this introduction I started and conducted the project from scratch in the company based on SoTA papers I found.

I thus created alone during this intership the full pipeline for MRI processing: from datasets downloading, merging and processing to uploading trained deep learning models automatically on MlFlow. I experimented various ways of preprocessing MRIs and different model architectures in order to give as many interpretable results as possible at the end of my internship.

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • Pytorch
  • Pandas
  • h5py
  • Numpy
